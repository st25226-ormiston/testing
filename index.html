My favourite sport is football

Football (or soccer) is the world's most popular sport, featuring two teams of 11 players aiming to score by moving a ball into the opposing net, primarily using their feet. Governed by FIFA, it is a 90-minute game (split into two 45-minute halves) played on a field with, or without, the use of hands, except for the goalkeeper.
